# Analysis

## Layer 1, Head 3

The attention of masked word is deeply relied on the bang mark "!" 

Example Sentences:
- I love [mask]!
- I love [MASK] 'cause cat is so cute!

## Layer 3, Head 7

The masked word is inferred from prep "on".

Example Sentences:
- There is a [MASK] on the table.
- I saw a [MASK] on the wall.
